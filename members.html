Our Team

Meet our diverse team of researchers, including postdocs, PhD students, and master's students, all working together to advance our understanding of schizophrenia.

Group Leader

Dr. Yun-Shuang Fan

Postdoctoral Fellow

Fan specializes in fMRI data analysis and computational modeling of brain networks. Her current work focuses on identifying functional connectivity biomarkers in schizophrenia.

PhD Students

Liju Liu

PhD Candidate

Alex's research focuses on applying graph theory and machine learning to fMRI data to identify novel biomarkers for schizophrenia. He is also interested in translational neuroscience.

Yiqi Mi

PhD Candidate

Emma is investigating the role of genetic factors in functional brain connectivity using multimodal imaging and GWAS data. Her work aims to bridge genomic and neuroimaging findings.
