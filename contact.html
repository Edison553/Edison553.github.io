Our Location

How to Find Us

Our lab is located in the Life Science and Technology Building on the main campus of the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. We are on the 5th floor, Room 503.

By Public Transport

The nearest subway station is Line 2, Xipu Station (Exit A), which is approximately a 15-minute walk from the campus. Buses 116, 221, and 311 also stop near the university.

By Car

Parking is available on campus. Please use the main entrance on Xiyuan Avenue and follow the signs to the Life Science and Technology Building.
