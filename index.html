Our Research Objectives

Discover Disease Patterns

Using fMRI data and advanced image processing techniques to identify distinct neural patterns associated with schizophrenia.

Biomarker Identification

Developing and validating novel biomarkers for early detection, prognosis, and personalized treatment strategies.

Multi-modal Integration

Integrating neuroimaging data with genetic information (GWAS, SNP) to uncover the complex interplay between genes and brain function.

Clinical Translation

Translating basic research findings into clinical applications to improve diagnosis and treatment of schizophrenia.
