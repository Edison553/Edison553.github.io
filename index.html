About Our Group

Our Mission

The Brain Imaging and Schizophrenia Group focuses on using advanced fMRI techniques and computational methods to study the neural basis of schizophrenia and other brain disorders. Our research aims to improve diagnosis, treatment, and prevention strategies through innovative neuroimaging analysis.

We are part of the "Brain Imaging and Pattern Recognition Laboratory" led by Professor Chen Huafu at the School of Life Science and Technology, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC).
